Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Fluffypaw: Cute Pet Jigsaw Puzzle ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile application.

Summary: Fluffypaw does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All game data is stored locally on your device only.

1. Information We Collect

Fluffypaw does not collect any personal information from users. We do not require account creation, login, or any form of registration.

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Game progress (cleared stages, current stage, frame collection)
  • In-game coin balance
  • Sound and other app settings

This local data is never transmitted to us or any third party. It exists solely to provide the game experience and is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers used for advertising
  • Contacts, photos, or other personal files
  • Payment or financial information (the app has no in-app purchases)
  • Analytics or usage statistics

3. Permissions

Fluffypaw does not request any sensitive runtime permissions (such as camera, microphone, contacts, location, or storage access).

4. Third-Party Services

Fluffypaw does not integrate any third-party advertising networks, analytics services, or social media SDKs. The app does not display ads.

5. Children's Privacy

Fluffypaw is suitable for all ages, including children. Since we do not collect any personal information from any user, including children under 13, the app fully complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

6. Data Security

Because all game data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted, there is no risk of network-based data breaches affecting your information. Local device security is managed by your device's operating system.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Material changes will also be communicated through the app's update notes.
